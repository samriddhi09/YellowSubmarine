MERMAID SUIT

The new underwater world is unprecedented and daunting, and you aren't familiar with handling heavy underwater vehicular equipment. The Mermaid Suit is the next best thing after an actual submarine. With mechanical tail, it will glide you through the water without any pre- requisite expertise, whether you are late for work or fleeing from a sea monster.

SEAMOTH

The Seamoth is a small, versatile one-person submersible capable of sustained high-speed travel over considerable distances. It boasts an omnidirectional propulsion system that allows for lateral and vertical movement in any single direction. It can be fitted with multiple upgrades and modifications that enhance performance and durability.

YELLOW SUBMARINE

Yellow Submarine is a new exclusive limited time submarine. It's an in-house special, based on the classic song Yellow Submarine by The Beatles. The submarine has custom skin interior and exterior. Buy before its gone. It gradually increases to a maximum of 11.25 m/s in any single direction. It comes with extra customizable add-on modules, making it idea for littoral sea exploration.

PRAWN SUIT

The Pressure Reactive Armored Waterproof Nano Suit Mk.III (abbv. Prawn Suit Mk.III) is a bipedal mechanical walker, designed for use in extreme-pressure and zero-gravity environments. A plasteel-reinforced canopy of enameled glass protects the single occupant. It has a base crush depth of 900 meters and can be upgraded to withstand a maximum depth of 1,700 meters.

CYCLOPS

The Cyclops is equipped with a vehicle bay capable of storing a Seamoth or Prawn Suit and can be modified with a range of different upgrades. It is capable of diving to depths of 500 meters, or 1700 meters fully upgraded. It can be utilized as a mobile base, capable of being outfitted with furniture and other amenities using the Habitat Builder.

ATLANTIS

The Atlantis is the largest vehicle in the ocean. Being a upgrade of cyclops Atlantis is near perfection. Sitting at 70m long Atlantis has all the space you will ever need. Customizable upgrades are also available making it your next home which you can carry anywhere you want.

NEPTUNE

If you are a fan of exploring the outer worlds, the Neptune Escape Rocket is your final and most precious masterpiece; your one way ticket out of here. It can take you to any part of the solar system you wish. The preliminary step toward humans becoming extra-terrestrial beings. It can carry up to 4 people.
